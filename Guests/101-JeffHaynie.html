#101 Jeff Haynie on thinking under pressure, contracting and entrepreneurship

Jeff is co-founder and CEO at Pinpoint, helping companies to build software better. Previously, Jeff co-founded and scaled Appcelerator to acquisition by Axway in 2016. Jeff is a long-time serial entrepreneur and angel investor. Prior to Appcelerator, Jeff was Co-founder and CTO of Vocalocity, and CTO of digital incubator eHatchery. He has worked on numerous standard committees from IETF to W3C, and contributed to several open source technologies including JBoss and OpenVXI. At the beginning of his career, Jeff served with distinction in the U.S. Navy. Jeff first told us how he went from coding games for fun, to selling software as a teenager, to solving problems on the deck of an aircraft carrier. We then talked about contracting and running a contracting business, and drifted toward entrepreneurship. Jeff finally told us the birth story of Titanium in 2006 and his new startup pinpoint.
